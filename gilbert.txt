From I-35 exit 113 (Ames 13th Street) drive west 2 1/2 miles to Grand Ave.  Turn right (north).  Grand Avenue is also US route 69.  Drive north out of Ames 5 miles to the Casey's at 170 Street.  Turn right (west).  Go through the roundabout and to the stop sign.  Turn left (south).  The school is just ahead and the parking lot is to the right.
