Smith's Dancing Meadows, 1503 190th St., Waverly
Smith's Dancing Meadows is in Bremer County, north of Waverly.  From US route 218 from Cedar Falls go around Waverly and about 5 miles north to 190th street.  East 4 miles past Euclid Ave.  Look for a sign on the north side of 190th.
